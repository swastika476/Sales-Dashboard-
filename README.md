   # Sales-Dashboard-
1.Sales Analytics:Superstore Sales Insights Dashboard
A dynamic,interactive data visualisation tool built to explore the superstore sales data-focusing on regional comparisons,based on categories,customer segments,payment modes,and shipping methods.it helps management  monitor KPIs and identify business trends.

2.Description 
Sales Analytics Dashboard is a visually engaging and anaytical Power BI report designed to help users explore and compare over regions.The dashboard focuses on highlighting.This dashboard is designed to monitor overall sales performance and identify business trends using interactive visualizations and KPIs. It enables managers to quickly analyze sales, profit, customer segments, regions, and product categories, helping them make informed business decisions.

3.Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
Power BI Desktop- Main data visualisation platform used for report creation.
Power Query -Data Transformaion  and cleaning layer for reshaping and preparing the data.
DAX(Data Analysis Expressions)-Used for calculated measures,dynamic visuals,and conditional logic.
Data Modeling -Relationships  established among tables(resorts,snow and data_distionary ) to enable  cross-filtering
File format-.pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews. 

4.Data Source
From Kaggle platform ,contain~5000 records ,including details on their region ,categories,payment mode,shipment mode,profit,quantity.

5.Business Problem
The business lacked a centralized view of its sales performance, making it difficult to monitor revenue, profitability, regional performance, product trends, and customer purchasing behavior. Decision-makers needed an interactive dashboard to quickly identify high-performing areas, detect declining trends, and make data-driven decisions.


6.Goal of the Dashboard
The primary goal of this dashboard is to provide a single source of truth for monitoring sales performance across different business dimensions. It enables stakeholders to analyze KPIs, compare year-over-year performance, identify top-performing products and regions, and evaluate customer segments, payment methods, and shipping preferences.

7.Walkthrough the key visual 
Sales (1.57M): Displays the total revenue generated.
Quantity (22K): Shows the total number of products sold.
Profit (175.26K): Indicates overall business profitability.
Average Shipment (4 Days): Tracks average delivery time to monitor operational efficiency.

1.Region Slicer
Allows users to filter the entire dashboard by Central, East, South, and West regions for regional analysis.

2.Monthly Sales by Year (Line Chart)
Compares monthly sales between 2019 and 2020.
Helps identify seasonality, growth trends, and months with peak or low sales.

3.Monthly Profit by Year (Line Chart)
Shows monthly profit trends across two years.
Helps determine whether increased sales are translating into higher profitability.

4.Sales by Category (Bar Chart)
Compares sales across major product categories:
Office Supplies
Technology
Furniture
Helps identify the highest revenue-generating category.

5.Sales by Sub-Category (Bar Chart)
Displays top-performing sub-categories such as Phones, Chairs, and Binders.
Useful for inventory planning and product strategy.
6.Sales by Segment (Donut Chart)
Breaks sales into:
Consumer
Corporate
Home Office
Identifies the most valuable customer segment.
7.Sales by Payment Mode (Donut Chart)
Shows customer payment preferences:
COD
Online
Cards
Supports payment optimization and customer experience improvements.
8.Sales by Region (Donut Chart)
Compares sales contribution across West, East, Central, and South regions.
Helps management identify strong and weak markets.
9.Sales by Ship Mode (Bar Chart)
Analyzes customer preference for shipping methods:
Standard Class
Second Class
First Class
Same Day
Supports logistics and delivery optimization.

This dashboard enables business leaders to:

Monitor sales and profit in real time.
Identify high-performing products and categories.
Compare year-over-year sales and profit trends.
Understand customer buying behavior.
Optimize inventory based on top-selling products.
Improve logistics using shipment insights.
Make faster, data-driven business decisions through interactive filtering.
